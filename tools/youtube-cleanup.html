Unsubscribe Script

⚠ This script only unsubscribes channels you marked Remove. Channels marked Keep, Unsure, or Unreviewed are completely ignored.

  1. Go to youtube.com/feed/channels — must be this exact page
  2. Make sure you are logged into your YouTube account
  3. Press F12 (Windows) or Cmd + Option + J (Mac) to open DevTools
  4. Click the Console tab — type allow pasting and press Enter if prompted
  5. Paste the script below and press Enter — it will only click channels in your Remove list
  6. Leave the tab open until it prints ✅ Done!
